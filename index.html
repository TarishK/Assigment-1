Interest in IT

IT, or information technology, is the use of computers and other technology to manage and process information. This can include a wide range of activities, such as storing, retrieving, and manipulating data, as well as more complex tasks like programming, networking, and security. The main reason I chose IT is that it gives me the opportunity to learn something new every day.Working closely with the IT & security department in my workplace is originally what sparked my interest and is how I found out about this course. Technology is ever evolving and so are the threats that come with it toward our security. Staying current with this and stopping bad actors is my main end goal for me.

Description

The position above is for a Security analyst role in cooperation. This role is a very technical role and is something that is appealing to me, due to the nature of the work and having the ability to set out and maintain a security infostructure of a whole business or organization.
